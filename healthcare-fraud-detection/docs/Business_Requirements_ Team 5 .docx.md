# **Business Requirements – Insurance Claim Fraud Detection**

**Project Objective:** Develop a system that identifies potentially fraudulent insurance claims before payout, helping reduce financial losses and improve claim processing.

## **Business Requirements**

1\. Detect fraudulent insurance claims before payout.

2\. Assign a fraud risk score (Low/Medium/High or 0–100%) to every claim.

3\. Flag high-risk claims for manual investigation.

4\. Enable faster approval of genuine claims.

5\. Provide reasons for why a claim was flagged as suspicious.

6\. Offer a dashboard showing claim statistics, fraud trends, and investigation status.

7\. Send notifications for high-risk claims.

8\. Protect customer data and maintain an audit log.

9\. Process claims quickly with high accuracy and minimal false alarms.

## **Expected Outcome**

Reduce fraudulent payouts, improve operational efficiency, lower investigation costs, and increase customer satisfaction by ensuring genuine claims are processed faster.
